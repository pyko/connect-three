How to play

Use the arrow keys to slide the tiles. Connect three or more same coloured tiles in a vertical or horizontal line to remove tiles from the board.

Works best in Chrome and Safari. Firefox still needs some style wrangling. Not tested in IE.

Created by pyko, with heavy inspiration from 2048.
